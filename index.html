Tips to maintain your Gi

  • MUST WASH YOUR GI AFTER EVERY USE
  • Hang-drying reccomended in most cases
  • Never wash or dry on high unless attempting to shrink
  • Possible buy a size up to intentionally shrink it down to your size so you aren't as worried about shrinkage in the future
  • If possible, own multiple gis so you aren't training in the same one every day
  • Wash gi inside out
  • If washing with rashguards, put rashguards in a laundry bag
